Credits

Angelina Angelic has performed in dozens of Czech and European cities such as Hamburg, Berlin, Zürich, Rome, Warsaw, Krakow, Bratislava and more. She has rich experience from numerous corporate and private events, vernissages and productions.

She has appeared in many TV and radio shows (highlights include Očima Josefa Klímy, Show Jana Krause, ČT art, Intim Night, Očko Drive, Darkwave, Radio FM) and various magazines (Burlesque Bible, Reflex, Týden, La Femme, Právo, Vlasta, 360°, Emma, and more).
