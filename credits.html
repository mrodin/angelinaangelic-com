Credits

Angelina Angelic has performed in dozens of Czech and European cities such as Hamburg, Berlin, Zürich, Rome, Warsaw, Krakow, Bratislava and more. She has rich experience from numerous corporate and private events, vernissages and big theatre productions.

She has appeared in many TV and radio shows (highlights include Očima Josefa Klímy, Show Jana Krause, ČT art, Intim Night, Óčko Drive, Darkwave, Radio FM) and various magazines (Burlesque Bible, Reflex, Týden, Glanc, La Femme, Právo, Vlasta, 360°, Emma, and more).

Some of the most notable clients and renowned venues she has performed for include:
Semperoper (Dresden), Firebirds Burlesque Show 2018, Mercy Brown (Krakow), Queen Calavera - The Home of Burlesque, Caput Mundi International Burlesque Award, Electro Swing Fever, Bizzare Carnival, Swing that night away, FAMU Fest Noir, Maserati & Ferrari Christmas party, Anglo-American University Alumni annual ball, Prague Fashion Weekend, Yes, Visage!, Buddha Bar Prague, show at Adrian T. Bell concert, Česká pojišťovna, Havas Worldwide Prague, Ogilvy & Mother, Blažek, Orgrez, PKNO, Kupfer Kabaret and many more.
