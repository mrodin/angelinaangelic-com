Acts

Lion Tamer

So is this where the fun ends or begins? With a cheeky spark in the eye and a whip which she isn’t afraid to use in her hand, this vixen is without a doubt fully equipped to tame the wildest of animals! Roaarr!! Angelina’s explosive signature act which was chosen for the Caput Mundi International Burlesque Award in Rome. Energetic, dynamic and funny. This act can be performed solo or with two side dancers upon request.

Aphrodite

Playfully cheeky Aphrodite has descended upon the Earth to spread beauty, love and naughty thoughts. A sixties cheesecake dance referencing act with a delightful powder puff finale.

Firebird

No cage can hold a firebird forever. Never!! Storming the stage in a spectacular flaming orange costume covered with large rhinestones and with a luxurious ostrich feather tail, Angelina celebrates her newly acquired freedom with a powerfully escalating striptease and a refined feather fan dance. Hot and saucy!
